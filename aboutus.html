Neil Engineering   About Us   Vision

Vision

Formation of NEIL ENGINEERING A group of engineers to build better tomorrow, is an advanced engineering design and BIM consultancy, as it is known today is earlier days was known as CIVIL SOLUTIONS.

CIVIL SOLUTIONS was found in 2016 in India and was providing ample civil engineering services domestically for better tomorrow. CIVIL SOULTIONS was mainly into providing services in Architectural & drafting services, survey design and small scaled land development projects. In the early 2019 CIVIL SOLUTIONS formed as a NEIL ENGINEERING This created a company with great vision to build better tomorrow and provide cost effective services to this modern civil engineering and BIM industry.

Today, NEIL ENGINEERING is providing ample civil engineering services and solutions including project management, resource management, design and BIM consultation for all types of infrastructure and non-infrastructure projects. NEIL ENGINEERING is also experts in Civil engineering, architectural and structural designs, land development, site development, Subdivision designs, Condotel developments, surface water management studies, drainage and sewage network design, survey design and boundary legal descriptions, surface/topography projects, drainage projects, street improvement projects CAD & BIM supports and preparation of CAD & BIM standards documentation for various region across the globe.
